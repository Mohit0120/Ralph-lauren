Ralph Lauren Spring 2025

For more than 50 years, Ralph Lauren has gathered with his family in the Hamptons. The natural beauty and sophisticated ease of his beloved seaside retreat inspire his Spring 2025 vision of coastal elegance and timeless American style


"THE HAMPTONS IS MORE THAN A PLACE..."

... It’s a natural world of endless blue skies, the ocean, green fields, and white fences, rusticity and elegance with a quality of light that drew artists here decades ago. It has been home, my refuge and always an inspiration.”

THE SHOW

Watch Now

A horse stable in a hamlet dotted with farm stands and shingled cottages was the backdrop for the World of Ralph Lauren Spring 2025 show. As horses grazed in green pastures and the sun softened in the salty sea air, Collection, Purple Label, and Polo showcased the Hamptons as Ralph Lauren sees it, with authentic style, rustic elegance, and family ties
